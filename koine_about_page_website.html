For individuals

How can students establish an enduring and effective personal knowledge management system (PKM)? How can students learn and discover the connections between knowledge and reality? How can students make informed decisions in their life concerning their future while choosing one educational institution over another?

For educators

How can teachers deliver their knowledge in a concise and unambiguous way? How can teachers free themselves up from the administrative tasks, from the delivering of documents and the explanation of no value-added information in order to focus on teaching? How can teachers manage the communication with their peers within their discipline and outside their discipline?

For institutions

How can institutions provide a meaningful knowledge base where individuals and educators can access anytime and anywhere? How can institutions share their content in order to improve the overall quality and efficiency of the education system? How can institutions help teacher, students, and other institutions to make informed decisions concerning their future?
